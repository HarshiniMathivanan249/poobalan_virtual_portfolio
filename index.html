Working Process

Concept

  • Video Editor
    Understand the project brief and objectives.
    Collaborate with the team to plan the storyline, structure, and style of the video.
    Gather references and determine the visual and auditory tone.
  • Graphic Designer
    Analyze the project requirements and target audience.
    Brainstorm ideas and explore different visual styles, colors, and themes.
    Create mood boards or sketches to present concepts.

Design

  • Video Editor
    Collect and organize raw footage, audio, and other assets.
    Start editing by cutting, trimming, and arranging clips according to the script or plan.
    Add effects, transitions, and animations to enhance the visual storytelling.
  • Graphic Designer
    Begin designing digital elements, such as logos, layouts, and illustrations.
    Use design software (e.g., Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop) to create and refine visual compositions.
    Integrate typography, color schemes, and branding elements.

Execute

  • Video Editor
    Finalize the video by polishing the edits, color grading, and syncing audio.
    Export the video in the required format and resolution.
    Deliver the final video to the client or team, making revisions if needed.
  • Graphic Designer
    Finalize designs by ensuring all elements align with the brief and brand guidelines.
    Prepare files for production (e.g., print, digital platforms).
    Present the completed designs to the client and make any necessary adjustments.
